Celebration of Surokkha Day

July 23, 2023

On the one-year anniversary of the creation of the "Surokkha" poem, Shuvo and Bishan planned a grand celebration. They reminisced about the birth of the poem, the memes that followed, and the humor that filled their conversations for months to come. They planned to recreate some of the most iconic moments and even considered writing a sequel to the original poem. It was meant to be a day of laughter, nostalgia, and appreciation for their friend Kobi's unique creativity.

Unfortunately, Kobi was unable to join the celebrations. Despite being the focal point of the festivities, circumstances prevented him from being a part of the day that was dedicated to his creation. Shuvo and Bishan felt his absence, but they decided to go ahead with the celebration, promising to fill Kobi in on all the details. The day was filled with laughter and reminiscing, but there was a palpable sense of incompleteness without Kobi's presence.
